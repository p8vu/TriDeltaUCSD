Formal Recruitment
Why Tri Delta? Tri Delta offers you a lifelong bond of friendship with women who are share similar aspirations and goals.

In Tri Delta, you are a unique and valuable part of a connected whole. We don't want you to come here and be like us. We want you to come here and be you.

Current Open Bidding
Current Open Bidding (COB) is recruitment that happens outside of Fall Formal recruitment. It is a more informal recruitment process, which allows potential new members to get to know current members better.

Events include beach bonfires, lunch at the Farmer's Market, and frozen yogurt at Yogurtworld.

Our chapter usually has COB during Winter and/or Spring quarter, but we currently are not doing COB this Spring Quarter!
Welcoming Our New Members
Acceping a bid from Tri Delta was one of the most exciting memories we've experienced at UCSD.

We know that the recruitment process can be overwhelming, but after accepting your bid, our sisters will immediately welcome you into the sisterhood. We've prepared many fun bonding events, like Big-Little Week, Beach Bonfires, and Movie Nights that you can look forward to after you join!
