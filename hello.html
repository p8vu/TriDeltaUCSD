"The glory of life is to give, not to be given; to love, not to be loved; to serve, not to be served."

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital
Do something that matters. Since the early 1970s Tri Delta’s chosen philanthropy has been children’s cancer charities. In 1999, Tri Delta formed a national philanthropic partnership with St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital to support their mission of finding cures and saving children.
Delta Idol
Love to sing? Every year, we host a a fun talent show, where all the sororities and fraternities perform in a singing competition! This campaign is our most successful event across the nation and raises the most amount of money for St. Jude.
External Philanthropy
Give back to your community. We take as much pride in our support of other fraternities and sororities as we do with our own. As sisters of TriDelta, we participate in external events hosted by other members of the Greek community to help raise money and awareness for their organizations.
